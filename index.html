About

A spinoff from the German Cancer Research Center (DKFZ), aiPTO harnesses the disruptive Individualized Patient Tumor Organoid (IPTO) technology which replicates tumor microenvironments at the molecular level and is predictive of clinical outcomes. It can be applied to a wide range of tumors of the central nervous system, from aggressive brain tumors such as glioblastomas to brain metastases.

aiPTO's proprietary generative AI technology with multiple task-specific AI agents is trained on the unique single cell sequencing data generated from IPTO. The AI agents are shown to be able to predict drug response on the single cell level, predict mechanism of action, and anticipate drug resistance and counter-resistance strategy.

By integrating the best biological model and the best AI model, aiPTO creates a powerful platform that tailors brain cancer therapy to the unique biology of each patient. This synergy redefines personalized medicine by improving drug efficacy, predicting resistance before it emerges, and reducing the time and cost of clinical development.

Our Technology

IPTO Platform

Our Individualized Patient Tumor Organoid (IPTO) technology creates miniature 3D replicas of patient brain tumors. These organoids preserve the complex cellular architecture and genetic profile of the original tumor.

  • Recreates tumor microenvironment
  • Preserves patient-specific cellular diversity
  • Enables high-throughput drug screening
  • Generates rich single-cell sequencing data

Generative AI Platform

Our proprietary AI platform leverages multiple specialized agents to analyze complex data from our organoid models. The system generates detailed predictions about drug responses and resistance mechanisms.

  • Predicts drug response at single-cell level
  • Identifies mechanisms of action
  • Anticipates resistance development
  • Suggests counter-resistance strategies
