About

A spinoff from the German Cancer Research Center (DKFZ), aiPTO harnesses the disruptive Individualized Patient Tumor Organoid (IPTO) technology which replicates tumor microenvironments at the molecular level and predicts clinical outcomes for brain cancer patients.

Our proprietary AI platform analyzes single-cell sequencing data from these organoids to predict drug responses, identify mechanisms of action, and anticipate drug resistance patterns. This integrated approach enables truly personalized brain cancer therapy with improved efficacy and reduced time to treatment.

IPTO: Brain organoid (green) with tumor cells (red). Source: Liu/DKFZ

Our Technology

IPTO Platform

Our Individualized Patient Tumor Organoid (IPTO) technology creates 3D replicas of patient brain tumors that preserve the complex cellular architecture and genetic profile of the original tumor.

  • Recreates tumor microenvironment
  • Enables high-throughput drug screening
  • Generates rich single-cell sequencing data

AI Platform

Our proprietary AI platform analyzes complex data from our organoid models to generate detailed predictions about drug responses and resistance mechanisms.

  • Predicts drug response at single-cell level
  • Identifies mechanisms of action
  • Anticipates resistance and suggests counter-strategies
